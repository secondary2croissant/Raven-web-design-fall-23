Is Turf Causing More Injuries in the NFL?

Since 2023 the NFLs fields are split 50% turf and 50% grass but this is coming with new found problems

The main problems these are causing is a 16% increase in lower body injuries but a 27% injury increase in non contact injuries.

Why would they want turf fields?

The simple answer to this is cost effencicy. The average cost to install a turf field is around 1 million but the upkeep is around 8,000$ a year. On the contuary a grass field cost 600,000$ to install but requires around 30,000$ dollars worth of upkeep a year.
