Firefighters

Description: Firefighteres respond to alarms, medical emergencies, hazardous materials, urban rescue, and other calls to protect life and property.

Average Salary: 42k-120k per year

Employers:

  • Federal agencies
  • state institutions
  • tribes and private contractors

Related Careers:

  • Emergency Medical Technicians: medical professional that gives emergency care
  • Fire inspectors and investigators: investigates fire hazards and code violations
  • EMTs/Paramedics: respond to calls to provide treatment, life support, or care to patients

Detailed information

Control and extinguish fires or respond to emergency situations where life, property, or the environment is at risk. Duties may include fire prevention, emergency medical service, hazardous material response, search and rescue, and disaster assistance.
